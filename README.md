# Analysing the Impact of Climate Risk on Business Survival: A Data Mining-Based Predictive Model

## Project Overview

This project analyzes how climate risks such as temperature anomalies, rainfall deviations, floods, and heatwaves affect the survival of Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) in India.

Using Machine Learning and Survival Analysis techniques, the project predicts SME distress and identifies key climate risk drivers through Explainable AI (SHAP).

---

## Research Objectives

- Analyze the impact of climate variables on SME survival.
- Develop predictive models using Random Forest, XGBoost, and LightGBM.
- Estimate SME survival probability under different climate scenarios.
- Identify key climate risk drivers using SHAP.
- Develop a district-level climate risk assessment framework.

## Key Findings

- Temperature anomaly was the strongest predictor of SME failure.
- Extreme rainfall increased business distress risk.
- Flood events significantly impacted business continuity.
- XGBoost achieved the best predictive performance.
- SHAP identified temperature anomaly as the most influential variable.
- Future warming scenarios increased SME distress rates.
